Featured Case Studies

01
Cyber Financial Crime

Bangladesh Bank SWIFT Heist Investigation

In February 2016, attackers exploited vulnerabilities in Bangladesh Bank's infrastructure to issue 35 fraudulent SWIFT transfer requests totaling nearly $1 billion. $81 million was successfully siphoned. The attack, later attributed to the Lazarus Group, exploited the absence of firewalls and unmonitored connections. Contributed to forensic analysis, post-incident assessment, and remediation framework development.
